Waraqah — Quran Video Studio, v0.1 (offline build)

1. Create a project.
2. In the Qur'an tab, open a surah and tap + next to an ayah to place it on the timeline.
3. Select a clip on the timeline to style it in the right-hand panel — font size, colour, alignment, background and animation.
4. Drag clips to move them, drag the right edge to change duration.
5. Add images, video or audio from the Media tab.
6. Press Export to render a real .webm file from the canvas.
